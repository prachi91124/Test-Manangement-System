Test Management System

A Test Management System (TMS) is a software tool that provides a comprehensive platform for managing the entire software testing process. It helps testing teams to plan, design, execute and report on their tests, as well as track and manage defects throughout the testing cycle. A TMS allows testing teams to create and maintain test cases, assign them to testers, and schedule test runs. It also enables teams to track the progress of testing, generate reports and analytics, and communicate with stakeholders about the testing status. A well-designed TMS can significantly improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the testing process, and ultimately contribute to the overall quality of the software being developed.
