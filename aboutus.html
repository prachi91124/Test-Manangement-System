About Us

This Test Management System is designed by our team to help schools colleges and various other institutions, to organize an exam without much hassle. As it was witnessed by everyone during pandemic, taking exams offline and offline exam organization was very difficult for many and many institutions were facing a lot of problems to organize online exams in a fair way.

So we decided to design a portal to help many such institutions and also to store results.

As we can see that our project is also environment friendly as it saves paper in large amounts and also takes care of workforce by keeping them tension free. Our portal is hassle free and tension free.
